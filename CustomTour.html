Golden Triangle Tour

The Golden Triangle is India’s leading tourism circuit — for a reason. This “triangle” of destinations includes the historic city of Delhi, which boasts three UNESCO World Heritage sites; Agra, home of the Taj Mahal, the world’s most beautiful building; and Jaipur, the Pink City, capital of Rajasthan. We also include some offbeat, unique, and authentic experiences. The India for Beginners Golden Triangle itinerary can be customized to your dates, preferences, and budget.

Shopping in India Tour

India is a shopper’s paradise. There is an astonishing variety of dazzling clothes, fabrics, shawls, shoes, jewellery, spices, rugs, pottery, furniture, and much, much more to buy in India. We are shopping experts and we can take you to some of the best stores, workshops, and showrooms in Delhi and Jaipur. The tour is designed to be a fun and interactive cultural experience: you will learn a lot about items such as pashmina shawls, silk sarees, block print fabric, and hand-knotted rugs, along with the culture that inspired them!

Fabulous Rajasthan: Land of Kings & Queens

Fabulous Rajasthan: Land of Kings and Queens is about immersion into the world of classical India. This one is for people who want to see the India depicted in tourist brochures — the fairy-tale like forts and palaces, loping camels and dazzling peacocks. The itinerary includes the Golden Triangle and diving into historic Delhi, visiting the Taj Mahal, exploring classical Rajasthan, including Jaipur, the Pink City, and romantic Udaipur, and a sojourn at a very special heritage lodge in rural Rajasthan.
