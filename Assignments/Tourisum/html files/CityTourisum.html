Ambala offers several tourist places including religious sites like Gurudwaras and temples, as well as historical landmarks, parks, and vibrant shopping areas. Some notable places include Gurudwara Manji Sahib, Panjokhra Sahib, Rani Ka Talab, and Ambika Devi Temple.

Most Attractive Places in Ambala

Famous Gurudwaras in Ambala

  • Gurudwara Manji Sahib – dedicated to Guru Hargobind Ji
  • Gurudwara Panjokhra Sahib – located in Panjokhra village
  • Sis Ganj Gurudwara – known for architectural beauty
  • Lakhnaur Sahib – located in Lakhnaur village

Famous Temples in Ambala

  • Ambika Devi Temple – believed to be the origin of Ambala's name
  • Rani Ka Talab – historic site with Devi and Shiva temples

Shopping in Ambala

  • Jaggi City Center – local shopping landmark
  • Zudio – affordable fashion destination
  • Vishal Mega Mart – popular for clothing

Parks in Ambala

  • Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Park
  • Sardar Patel Park
  • Herbal Park

Food & Cuisine

  • Chole Bhature and Lassi from local dhabas
  • Bheega Kulcha and Tikki from street vendors
  • Jain Sweets for traditional mithai like Gulab Jamun

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: October to March
  • Language: Hindi, Punjabi, and English are widely spoken
  • Transport: Auto-rickshaws, cabs, and e-rickshaws are commonly available

Nearby Attractions

  • Kurukshetra – Mythological and spiritual town (60 km)
  • Morni Hills – The only hill station near Ambala (70 km)
  • Chandigarh – Capital city with modern architecture (45 km)
