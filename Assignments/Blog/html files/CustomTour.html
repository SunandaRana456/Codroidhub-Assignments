Golden Triangle Tour

The Golden Triangle is India’s leading tourism circuit — for a reason. This “triangle” of destinations includes the historic city of Delhi, Agra (home of the Taj Mahal), and Jaipur, the Pink City. We also include offbeat and authentic experiences. The itinerary can be customized to your dates, preferences, and budget.

Shopping in India Tour

India is a shopper’s paradise. We can take you to some of the best stores, workshops, and showrooms in Delhi and Jaipur. Learn about pashmina shawls, silk sarees, block print fabric, and hand-knotted rugs along with the culture that inspired them.

Fabulous Rajasthan: Land of Kings & Queens

This tour immerses you in classical India: fairy-tale forts, palaces, camels, and peacocks. Explore Delhi, the Taj Mahal, Jaipur, Udaipur, and stay at a heritage lodge in rural Rajasthan.
